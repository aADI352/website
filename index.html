About Blog

Engineering undergraduate and Aerospace enthusiast

My name is Adi Atrash, I'm a second year undergraduate student at the Technion ‐ Israel Institute of Technology studying aerospace engineering. My passion lays in aerospace, but I'm obsessed with anything engineering :)

Since highschool, I have always been fascinated by the fields of fluid mechanics, propulsion and astronautics. Towards the end of my first year, I built my first ever interactive CFD solver to numerically solve the flow equations.

I am a very active student, both on and off campus. I am a member of the aerodynamics team in Technion's Formula Student club where I act as aerodynamics lead, in addition to being a member in the uni's rocketry club, specifically working on propulsion.

Off campus, I am a CAD mentor in my local FIRST robotic`s team, where I help high school students realise their ideas using SolidWorks and Onshape!

I am happy to share that I'm joining Technion's RTFL lab this summer as a research assistant!
